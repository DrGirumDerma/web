Our Services

General Dermatology

Diagnosis and treatment of common skin conditions like acne, eczema, psoriasis, rosacea, and fungal infections.

Cosmetic Dermatology

Advanced aesthetic treatments including Botox, dermal fillers, chemical peels, and laser therapies for anti-aging and skin rejuvenation.

Skin Cancer Screening

Comprehensive skin exams for early detection and prevention of skin cancer, including mole mapping and biopsies.

Pediatric Dermatology

Specialized care for infants, children, and adolescents with skin conditions such as birthmarks, warts, and pediatric eczema.

Hair & Nail Disorders

Diagnosis and treatment for various hair loss conditions, nail infections, and other related disorders.

Advanced Laser Treatments

Utilizing state-of-the-art laser technology for scar revision, pigmentation issues, and vascular lesions.

About Dr. Girum Dermatology Clinic

At Dr. Girum Dermatology Clinic, we are dedicated to providing the highest quality dermatological care in a compassionate and welcoming environment. Led by Dr. Girum, a board-certified dermatologist with years of experience, our clinic offers a comprehensive range of services for all your skin, hair, and nail concerns.

Our philosophy is centered on patient education and personalized treatment plans. We believe in empowering our patients with knowledge about their skin conditions and involving them in every step of their care journey. We utilize the latest advancements in dermatological science and technology to ensure effective and safe outcomes.

Whether you're seeking treatment for a chronic skin condition, interested in cosmetic enhancements, or need a thorough skin cancer screening, Dr. Girum and our dedicated team are here to provide expert care tailored to your unique needs.
